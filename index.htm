Novel Android App

This is an android app I designed that allows you to scan books and gives you important information like the average rating, title, author, number of pages, and a little description about the book. I love to read and sometimes I just peruse my local bookstore for interesting finds that I may not want to buy at the time but need a way of remembering them for later.

*The book search feature is powered by the Google Books API and the barcode scanning by Scandit.
